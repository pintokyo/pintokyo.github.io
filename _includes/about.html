About

Gvero's Pintokyo Games is a game studio and development company placed in Lausanne, Switzerland. The main services of the company are video game development and commercialization, and development of software which includes IT applications and software tools.

Tihomir Gvero, the founder of the company, has more than ten years of experience in software development. He worked as a researcher at EPFL, as a consultant at Itecor and as a data scientist at Credit Suisse. He holds a PhD in computer science from EPFL.
